Usage: @GetAfniView <Name> .....
example: @GetAfniView /Data/stuff/Hello+orig.HEAD
returns the afni view of Name (+orig)
Wildcards are treated as regular characters:
example: @GetAfniView 'AAzst1r*+orig'
returns : +orig
