3dSeg segments brain volumes into tissue classes.

Examples: (All examples can do without the -gold* options)
  Case A: Segmenting a T1 volume with a brain mask available
  A.1:  Brain mask and MRF only.
  3dSeg    -anat banat+orig     \
           -mask anat.ns+orig   \
           -gold goldseg+orig   -gold_bias goldbias+orig   \
           -classes 'CSF ; GM ; WM' \
           -Bmrf 1.0            \
           -bias_classes 'GM ; WM' -bias_fwhm 25 \
           -prefix case.A.1  -overwrite    \

  A.2:  Adding average mixing fraction constraint derived from
        population based spatial priors, and preserving the weighting
        throughout the segmentation.
  3dSeg    -anat banat+orig     \
           -mask anat.ns+orig   \
           -gold goldseg+orig   -gold_bias goldbias+orig   \
           -classes 'CSF ; GM ; WM' \
           -bias_classes 'GM ; WM' -bias_fwhm 25 \
           -prefix case.A.2  -overwrite    \
           -mixfrac WHOLE_BRAIN \
           -Bmrf 1.0 -main_N 4           \
