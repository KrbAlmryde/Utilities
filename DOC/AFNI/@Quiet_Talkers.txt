A program to kill AFNI/SUMA talking programs
ps is used to lookfor processes running the programs:
afni 3dGroupInCorr plugout_drive suma DriveSuma
that also have the -npb option used in their command line.

Examples:
   @Quiet_Talkers

   @Quiet_Talkers -npb_val 3 -npb_val 6

   @Quiet_Talkers -npb_range 5 9
